About the Aptitude Course

The pre-recorded 24-session Aptitude Course is an integral component of the NAO, designed to significantly enhance the participants' skills in problem-solving, logical reasoning, and verbal ability.

Version 1

Grades 8 & 9

Version 2

Grades 10 & 11

Version 3

Grade 12

The course is divided into four main segments:

  • Skill Builder Orientation : 3 sessions
  • Foundations of Problem Solving : 7 sessions
  • Logical Reasoning Development : 7 sessions
  • Verbal Ability Enhancement : 7 sessions

How to Prepare for the Olympiad

Competing in the Olympiad with fellow students and doing well requires thorough preparation. Along with assistance in your academic pursuit, the Olympiad will also provide students with the opportunity to grab exciting rewards.

Preparation Tips:

  • Go through the brief syllabus and understand each unit
  • Organize your routine and make a suitable timetable
  • Analyze and estimate the difficulty level of the Olympiad
  • Practise MCQ questions from various sources
  • Explore and understand the rewards to set targets
