What makes CL Tuitions unique from the other Tuitions?

The program is a great package of both learning and testing your learning as it has incorporated the tests which helps students to understand their understanding and standing as compared to their peers at national, state, and local level.

This facility is not available when a child gets themselves enrolled in the local tuition center. This unique feature of our program makes it the best choice of the students and parents.

Parent's Journey in the Program

Parents play a very important role in a student's academic journey. More importantly in this age where they are going near to making big decisions for their careers, parents and students both need to be clear about their interests and options available to them. So we have planned a parent journey as well along with students.

Parent Mentor Interaction

There will be regular interaction with the mentors in order to keep a track of the progress and performance of the students.

Seminar/Workshop for Parents

Various Seminar/Workshop for parents have also been scheduled across the duration of the course, having parenting tips and career options on agenda.
