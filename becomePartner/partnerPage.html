Franchise Scope

In the modern era of rapid technological advancement, the demand for technically skilled professionals is at an all-time high. Competitive exams like SSC JE, State JE Exams, and CUET (for B.Tech and technical programs) are increasingly focusing not just on theoretical knowledge, but also on practical and application-based skills.

Every year, more than 15 lakh engineering graduates and around 5 lakh diploma holders pass out from over 10,000 technical institutions in India. This large pool of talent needs proper guidance and standardized training to match the evolving demands of both government and private sector job roles.

The job market is shifting — recruiters are now looking for candidates who can solve real-world problems, understand engineering drawings, apply basic design and software tools, and communicate technical concepts clearly.
